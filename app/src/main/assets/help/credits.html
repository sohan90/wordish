Sounds

Some sounds in Wordish come from soundjay.com or from pacdv.com who have graciously made them available under a Creative Commons license.

Dictionary

The definitions displayed in Wordish come from wiktionary.com.

The words allowed in the game come from the tournament word list commonly used for crossword-style games, and supplemented with longer words taken from Wiktionary.

The definitions were extracted from the file of articles Wiktionary makes available for download, under a Creative Commons license. We endeavored to make this extraction as error free as possible but some problems exist. In addition to other problems there are several hundred words included in the tournament word list that are not defined in Wiktionary. These appear to be mostly because of differing standards for what words qualify for inclusion. We regret that we have no definitions for these words.
